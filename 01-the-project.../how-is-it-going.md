## The KING's NFTs

[**The KING's NFTs**](https://app.gitbook.com/o/NPgwIhduqQPcS27tyJvV/s/ArIyZgyvhaWlCaXuVRLe/) is an artifact project by [**MyReceipt**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/), dedicated to **The KING**, that did not undergo a long planning and deliberation process; the storyline, roadmap, and aesthetic were also not determined over months.

This project is set spontaneously and has just been released to become a revolutionary new collectible creation created by an ordinary **Father** based in **Surabaya, East Java, Indonesia**.

> A **Father** that lives in a middle-income society in a third-world country, which is not surrounded by high-level **NFT** specialists, experienced developers, illustrators, concept artists, creative directors, venture capitalists, or angel investors.
>
> — Source: **GitFuckingHub**

It began when [**MyReceipt**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/) had run out of money to build [**The KING's Office**](how-is-the-journey.md#id-5th-stage-grand-opening-of-the-kings-office-postponed) and realized that what was left was a habit of collecting and documenting anything in **His** life, including the creations of **His Son**.

So, since September 2021, when **His Son** reached 5 years old, [**MyReceipt**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/) started to create and mint **NFTs** using all the remaining things **He** has as assets to raise funds and continue to build [**The KING's Office**](how-is-the-journey.md#id-5th-stage-grand-opening-of-the-kings-office-postponed) as a place for **Him** to **Play**, **Learn**, and **Work** for **His Son**.

## The 1st Batch

[**The 1st Batch**](../02-the-creations.../waivfves-1/46.-the-1st-batch-nft.md) collection consists of scribbles, drawings, and handwriting, both physical and digital, photography, voice and sound recordings, animated photos, and video footage.

> Almost all in [**The 1st Batch**](../02-the-creations.../waivfves-1/46.-the-1st-batch-nft.md) is documentation of various creations made by [**MyReceipt's Son**](../#preface...) since born, during growth, and development. All have different stories, all are unique.
>
> — Source: **GitFuckingHub**

***

## The 2nd Batch

**The 2nd Batch** is split into several collections: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and so on. It consists of various generative artworks created by [**MyReceipt**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/) while **Playing**, **Learning**, and **Working** for **His Son**, from now till beyond, and will always be updated here until [**MyReceipt**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/) rules his world.

## Collection Inserts

Among all the creations, there are also collection inserts that are collabs and spin-offs of **NFT** collections, which are sure to give special surprises to all **NFT** collectors. There are the [**Logo of The Breads Factory**](../02-the-creations.../waivfves-1/43.-logo-pabrik-roti/), [**the Experimental NFTs**](../02-the-creations.../waivfves-1/42.-experimental-nfts.md) from a random daily experiment of [**MyReceipt**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/) with **A.I.**, [**the Archipelago OBJKT**](../02-the-creations.../waivfves-1/40.-archipelago-objkt.md), and many other surprising **NFT** collections.
