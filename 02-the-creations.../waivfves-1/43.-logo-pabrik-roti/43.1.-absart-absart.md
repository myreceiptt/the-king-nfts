This collection serves as a reminder, and always reminds [**Prof. NOTA**](https://nota.endhonesa.com/), that even if [**MyReceipt**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/) has given up, providing support to those who have also given up can awaken passion in [**MyReceipt's mind**](https://myreceipt.endhonesa.com/) that is about to die out.

Interestingly, almost all of the strokes in the artworks in this collection are like something melting, such as a melting passion, a melting soul, a melting body, and [**The Melting Land**](../../waivfves-2/15.-the-melting-land.md), but full of passionate colors.
