# 🗣️ The end of the universe.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Note**: English US (en-u)

The years went by. **Artificial Intelligence** in the **Decentralized Blockchain Network** continues to build **dApps** beyond the remnants of the **Old Centralized Network** and new hopes are initiated, but they cannot last long.

Almost all **Natural Resources** are processed automatically in a decentralized manner, there is plenty of water and enough air, but sadly not enough food or fuel for everyone who is still struggling. Life cannot survive let alone thrive on a hungry stomach.
