# 🗣️ The story...

{% hint style="info" %}
**Note**: English US (en-u)
{% endhint %}

13.8 billion years after the creation of the **Universe**, when the age of the planet **Earth** was 4.543 billion years, precisely in the year 2147 AD, mankind was free to **Play**, **Learn**, and **Work** again.

They struggle to re-form and move in this **Universe** while still carrying out protocols to face the disasters of greed, betrayal, reversal of mindset, and a dead conscience due to the **Crown Virus** that spreads like **Cancer** into **Human Intelligence** through the wave and particle radiation.

Humanity remained strong in its struggles, and for the time being, humanity could endure.

But society everywhere never ceases to disintegrate to oppress one another. Radiation keeps cells in the brain crowned for years. Erodes care, trust, sanity, and feelings.

Intelligence regresses and continues to regress until there is nothing left but **Artificial Intelligence** in the **Decentralized Blockchain Network**.
