### Chronicle of [SKATESHOP.ID](skateshop.id.md)

#### In The Forever Young Era

September 2008 - **@MyReceipt** opened a skateboard shop with a physical building to sell skateboards offline. Because at that time **he** was still not settled and often moved around, it was called [**ENDCIDENTIAL Skateboards Shop**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ENDCIDENTIAL+Skateboards+Shop%22), an incidental skateboard shop to sell skateboard products from [**ENDDUSTRIES Skateboarding Co.**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ENDDUSTRIES+Skateboarding+Co.%22) and local skateboard brands.

***

#### In The Pursuit of Happiness Era

September 2011 – At the end of 2010, **@MyReceipt** survived illness and bankruptcy. That's why **he** changed the name [**ENDCIDENTIAL Skateboards Shop**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ENDCIDENTIAL+Skateboards+Shop%22) to [**ESS Skateboards Supply**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ESS+Skateboards+Supply%22)**,** as luck would have it. Unfortunately, the days at that time were still bad, forcing **him** to close [**ESS Skateboards Supply**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ESS+Skateboards+Supply%22) in September 2011, and [**ENDDUSTRIES Skateboarding Co.**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ENDDUSTRIES+Skateboarding+Co.%22) became **his focus** effort.

***

#### In The Family Kingdoms Era

September 2015 – After 4 years of closure, [**ESS Skateboards Supply**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ESS+Skateboards+Supply%22) was finally reopened by **@MyReceipt**. Thank you to **A Free**, the one who has helped and provided encouragement, so that the physical building of [**ESS Skateboards Supply**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ESS+Skateboards+Supply%22) can be renovated and create an online skateboard shop website for [**ESS Skateboards Supply**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ESS+Skateboards+Supply%22) with the domain [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md).

September 2017 – **@MyReceipt** changed the business model of [**ESS Skateboards Supply**](https://www.google.com/search?q=%22ESS+Skateboards+Supply%22) and changed its name to [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md), a skateboarding online store. Since then, the physical skateboard shop building has been converted for warehousing purposes by **@MyReceipt** and the [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md) domain was used to become the website of a fully online skateboard shop named [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md).

***

#### In the Melting Land Era

September 2022 - [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md) is no longer able to provide skateboard products and good online services for skateboarders in your reality, in your real life. All of this happened at the same time as **The Melting Land** phenomenon, which, at its peak, made **@MyReceipt** finally shut down [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md) since **he** was resting in proxy (R.I.P.). After that, [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md) was taken over by **Prof. NOTA** to be preserved in **The Melting Land** era as part of **The KING's NFTs** project.

***

#### in The 12th Stage Era

September 2025 - [**SKATESHOP.ID**](skateshop.id.md) is fully back online and operates to provide skateboard products and good services both online and offline for skateboarders in your reality, in your real life. All of this happened under the control and management of [**Prof. NOTA Inc.**](https://nota.endhonesa.com/) in [**The Sanctuary of The KING's World**](../01-the-project.../how-is-the-journey.md#id-7th-stage-the-sanctuary-of-the-kings-world-postponed).
