# 🤑 The Currencies

{% hint style="info" %}
**Note**: Depending on the **commodity**, the **digital currency**, the **cryptocurrency**, or the **Fungible Tokens**, the white paper should explain how the technology will work. By the end of this white paper, if you can't articulate what problem the [**$HAIL**](the-currencies.md), [**$OiOi**](the-currencies.md), and [**$NOTA**](the-currencies.md) solve and how it does so, then this white paper did a poor job. In fact, a well-articulated white paper is a sign of well-thought-out **Fungible Tokens** or **CryptoCurrencies**.&#x20;
{% endhint %}
